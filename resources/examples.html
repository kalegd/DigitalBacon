Loading From Templates

Loading from templates is quite easy, just follow the steps below

  • Download a template from this page
  • Start the Digital Bacon editor
  • Open the menu
    • Computer Users - Press the 'M' key on your keyboard
    • Mobile Users - Tap the 'OPEN MENU' button on the top right corner of your screen
    • VR Users - Press the primary button on either controller
        Example: for the Oculus Quest, the 'A' and 'X' buttons on the right and left controllers respectively
    • VR Hand Tracking Users - Face left palm towards you with fingers stretched open
  • Select 'Project File'
  • Select 'Load from Device'
  • Choose the template you downloaded in the first step
